How to Play

  • Place a disc to sandwich opponent discs between yours
  • All sandwiched discs flip to your color
  • Highlighted cells show your valid moves — you must play there
  • If you have no valid move, your turn is skipped
  • Game ends when the board is full or neither player can move
  • Most discs on the board wins!
